## The Problem

### E-commerce entrepreneurs face critical sourcing challenges:

| Pain Point | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| **Fragmented Sourcing** | Merchants spend 10-20 hours/week manually searching across 5+ supplier platforms |
| **Price Comparison Hell** | No easy way to compare prices, MOQs, and shipping across suppliers |
| **Supplier Verification** | High risk of scams and unreliable suppliers; no centralized trust scores |
| **Manual Data Entry** | Copy-pasting product details into Shopify is tedious and error-prone |
| **No Profit Visibility** | Difficult to calculate true margins with shipping, duties, and fees |

### Market Validation:
- **62% of dropshippers** cite "finding reliable suppliers" as their #1 challenge (Source: Oberlo Survey 2024)
- Average merchant spends **15+ hours/week** on product research
- **$4.8 trillion** global e-commerce market growing at 14% annually

## The Solution: SourceScout

### One Platform. All Suppliers. Smart Decisions.

SourceScout is the **"Kayak for Product Sourcing"**â€”aggregating supplier data and empowering merchants to make data-driven sourcing decisions in minutes, not hours.

### Core Features (MVP Complete âœ…)

#### 1. **Unified Multi-Source Search**
- Search Alibaba, Made-in-China, and CJ Dropshipping simultaneously
- Real-time results with product images, pricing, MOQ, and supplier ratings
- Smart filtering by source, price range, and minimum order quantity

#### 2. **Intelligent Product Comparison**
- Side-by-side comparison of up to 4 products
- Compare pricing, shipping, supplier ratings, and delivery times
- Calculate profit margins with customizable markup

#### 3. **One-Click Shopify Integration**
- Push products directly to Shopify as draft listings
- Automatic price markup calculation
- Metafields for supplier tracking and cost management
- Batch import multiple products

#### 4. **Save & Organize**
- Save products for later review
- Organize by collections or projects
- Track price changes over time (roadmap)

#### 5. **Export Flexibility**
- CSV export for Shopify bulk import
- Partner integrations (Dropified, Syncee) coming soon

## Technology Stack

| Layer | Technology | Rationale |
|-------|------------|-----------|
| **Frontend** | React 18 + TypeScript + Shopify Polaris | Native Shopify look & feel, enterprise-grade UI |
| **Backend** | Node.js + Express + TypeScript | Scalable, fast, type-safe |
| **Database** | PostgreSQL | Reliable, ACID-compliant, JSON support |
| **Caching** | Redis | Fast session and search caching |
| **Scraping** | Puppeteer | Robust web scraping for non-API sources |
| **Hosting** | Railway/Render (planned) | Cost-effective, scalable cloud deployment |

### Technical Differentiators:
- **Hybrid Data Approach:** Official APIs where available (CJ Dropshipping), intelligent scraping where not
- **Real-time Search:** Sub-3-second search across all sources
- **Shopify-Native:** Built as a Shopify app with OAuth integration
- **Mobile-Responsive:** Full functionality on tablet and mobile
